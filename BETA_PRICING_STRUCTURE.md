## Enterprise Clinic: Higher Deposit Strategy

### The Problem:
- **Enterprise Clinic**: 300 calls/day = 45,000 min/month
- **Vapi Cost**: 45,000 √ó $0.10 = $4,500/month
- **Beta Pricing**: $3,000-4,500/month
- **Issue**: Vapi cost = entire revenue (or more!)

### Solution: Higher Deposit to Offset Vapi Costs

#### Option 1: 3-Month Deposit
- **Monthly**: $4,000/month (beta)
- **Deposit**: $13,500 (3 months = $4,500 √ó 3)
- **Covers**: First 3 months of Vapi costs
- **After Month 3**: Profitable ($4,000 revenue - $4,500 Vapi = -$500, but deposit covers it)

#### Option 2: 6-Month Deposit (Recommended)
- **Monthly**: $4,000/month (beta)
- **Deposit**: $27,000 (6 months = $4,500 √ó 6)
- **Covers**: First 6 months of Vapi costs
- **After Month 6**: You've built voice layer, costs drop to $1,200/month
- **Profit**: $4,000 - $1,200 = $2,800/month ‚úÖ

#### Option 3: Annual Deposit (Best for You)
- **Monthly**: $4,000/month (beta)
- **Deposit**: $54,000 (12 months = $4,500 √ó 12)
- **Covers**: Entire first year of Vapi costs
- **Benefit**: You get cash upfront, can build voice layer faster
- **After Year 1**: Costs drop, highly profitable
